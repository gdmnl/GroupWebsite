Prof. Luo Siqiang's Group

I am a Nanyang Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University. I am also affiliated with DANTE. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in computer science from Fudan University, and a Ph.D. degree in computer science from the University of Hong Kong (working with Prof. Ben Kao and Prof. Reynold Cheng ). I also did a Postdoc at Harvard University (working with Prof. Stratos Idreos ).

Research Interest.

I am broadly interested in big data management algorithms and systems, particularly in the following areas:

1) Scalable graph algorithms/systems. Focus on optimizing fundamental graph analytical problems, such as PageRanks, GNNs and Community Search.

2) Scalable key-value systems. Focus on optimizing the performance of key-value stores such as RocksDB, particularly in filter and index designs.

3) Machine-learning driven data management.
